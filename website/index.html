What it does

Built around creator approval

Generate

Creates structured trivia scripts, answer choices, render manifests, and vertical MP4 drafts for creator review.

Review

Keeps a human approval gate before any upload workflow. Drafts can be approved, revised, rejected, or regenerated.

Prepare

Packages approved drafts with captions, hashtags, citations, and upload metadata for TikTok inbox/draft workflows.

TikTok use

Inbox upload, not automatic public posting

Trivia Clip Factory is designed to upload only creator-approved video drafts to TikTok inbox/drafts when official upload access is enabled. The creator remains responsible for final editing, posting, and compliance with TikTok policies.
