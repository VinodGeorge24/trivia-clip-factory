Privacy Policy

1. Overview

Trivia Clip Factory helps creators generate, review, and prepare short-form trivia video drafts. This policy explains what information may be processed when using the workflow.

2. Information Processed

Trivia Clip Factory may process creator-provided prompts, generated scripts, rendered draft video paths, captions, hashtags, source citations, review decisions, upload attempt records, and basic TikTok account authorization information when TikTok upload access is enabled.

3. TikTok Authorization

If the creator authorizes TikTok access, Trivia Clip Factory may use TikTok OAuth tokens only to support approved upload workflows, such as sending an approved MP4 draft to the creator's TikTok inbox or draft flow. Tokens should be stored securely and should not be published, shared, or committed to source control.

4. How Information Is Used

Information is used to operate the creator workflow: generating video drafts, preserving review history, preparing upload metadata, recording upload results, and improving future topic or format decisions.

5. Sharing

Trivia Clip Factory does not sell personal information. Information is shared with TikTok only when the creator authorizes TikTok access and uses an upload workflow. The creator may also choose to publish final videos through TikTok.

6. Retention And Control

Local workflow records may be retained for audit history, revision tracking, and creator operations. The creator can remove local runtime data and revoke TikTok authorization through TikTok account or app settings.

7. Security

Credentials, OAuth tokens, browser cookies, and session data should be handled as secrets. They should not appear in public pages, Telegram responses, logs, or committed repository files.

8. Contact

For privacy questions, contact triviaclips.vgeorge@gmail.com.
